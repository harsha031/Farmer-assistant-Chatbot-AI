Fertilizer Details

Phosphorus Fertilizer

Phosphorus from manure or sludge should be comparable to P from inorganic fertilizer.

Nitrogen Fertilizer

Photosynthesis of Starch and Cellulose Nitrogen is an essential element in the Photosynthesis of starch and cellulose in plants, so feeding the plants with nitrogen is very essential.

Potassium Fertilizer

The quality or grade of potassium fertilisers is expressed as a percentage of potassium oxide (K 2 O) equivalent.

Agricultural Waste

Fertilizers made from wastes Most fertilizers that are commonly used in agriculture contain the three basic plant nutrients: nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.

livestock manure

Most often, fresh manure is used as a nitrogen fertilizer while composted manure is used as a phosphorus fertilizer because phosphorus is approximately 80% available in both products.

Industrial Waste

Industrial waste is manufacturing waste from a wide range of different processes, such as sludges, product residues, kiln dust, slags, and ashes.
